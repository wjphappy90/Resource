El eje 'following' contiene todos los nodos del documento que se encuentran luego del nodo del contexto. Esto no incluye ancestros, descendientes, nodos de atributo ni nodos 'namespace'.
 
/AAA/XXX/following::*

     <AAA>
          <BBB>
               <CCC/>
               <ZZZ>
                    <DDD/>
                    <DDD>
                         <EEE/>
                    </DDD>
               </ZZZ>
               <FFF>
                    <GGG/>
               </FFF>
          </BBB>
          <XXX>
               <DDD>
                    <EEE/>
                    <DDD/>
                    <CCC/>
                    <FFF/>
                    <FFF>
                         <GGG/>
                    </FFF>
               </DDD>
          </XXX>
          <CCC>
               <DDD/>
          </CCC>
     </AAA>
